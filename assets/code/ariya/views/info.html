How to Book a Venue

  1. Search on Àríyá for event venues by location, name, available dates, price and much more

  2. Find the venues you love and book online immediately or enter a Bid race to save some money. Please note that to Book or Bid, you must register as a user

  3. As soon as your booking is confirmed or you win a bid you’ll get a notification. You can now invite your guests and have some Àríyá (/fun/).

Àríyá also provides services to host / announce / publicize your events online and send invitations to your guests
